# SOP: Authority Foundations
**Category:** 30 Day Sprint — Call 4 | **Duration:** 1h 40m

## Objective
Solidify the authority foundation — ensure all base-layer authority signals are established, verified, and generating measurable AI visibility improvements.

## Prerequisites
- Fulfillment Blueprint established (012)
- First 2 weeks of sprint tasks executed

## Step-by-Step Process

### Foundation Audit (Pre-Call)
1. Run full authority check across all 7 Authority Wheel spokes
2. Score each spoke (1-10) with evidence
3. Identify any spoke scoring below 5 — flag for immediate action
4. Prepare before/after comparison from sprint start

### Call Agenda
5. **Progress Review** (20 min)
   - Show moved metrics: citations created, reviews generated, pages optimized
   - Present before/after AI search comparison screenshots
   - Address any gaps
6. **Entity Foundation** (20 min)
   - Verify Knowledge Panel is claimed and accurate
   - Confirm Wikidata has 10+ verified statements
   - Ensure NAP is consistent across ALL discovered citations
7. **Content Foundation** (20 min)
   - Review published content: is it indexed? ranking?
   - Check schema on all new pages
   - Verify internal linking structure connects authority spokes
8. **Technical Foundation** (15 min)
   - Run Core Web Vitals check
   - Verify sitemap + robots.txt configuration
   - Check index coverage in Search Console
9. **Next Sprint Planning** (15 min)
   - Identify weak spokes for next optimization phase
   - Set targets for month 2

### Post-Call Actions
10. Fix any flagged foundation gaps within 48 hours
11. Begin optimization phase on weakest spokes
12. Generate month 1 report (before/after metrics)

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] All 7 spokes scored and documented
- [ ] Foundation gaps fixed
- [ ] Month 1 progress report delivered
- [ ] Month 2 optimization plan outlined

## Verification
All foundation spokes score 6+
Knowledge Panel accurately representing brand
AI search responses improving vs baseline
