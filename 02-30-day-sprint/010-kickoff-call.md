# SOP: 30-Day Sprint — Kickoff Call
**Category:** 30 Day Sprint | **Duration:** 2h 35m

## Objective
Launch the 30-Day AI Authority Sprint with proper client onboarding, expectation setting, and sprint roadmap delivery.

## Prerequisites
- Client signed up for sprint program
- AI Authority Wheel baseline established
- All Getting Started modules complete

## Step-by-Step Process

### Pre-Call Preparation
1. Review client's current AI authority baseline (from getting started modules)
2. Pull client's scorecard: Knowledge Panel status, review count, schema deployment
3. Prepare sprint roadmap document (30-day calendar)
4. Set up shared tracking dashboard (Google Sheets or LookerStudio)

### Call Agenda
5. **Welcome & Sprint Overview** (15 min)
   - What the 30-day sprint covers
   - Weekly commitment expectations
   - Success metrics definition
6. **Current State Review** (20 min)
   - Present baseline scorecard
   - Highlight biggest visibility gaps
   - Show competitor comparison
7. **Sprint Milestones** (20 min)
   - Week 1: Foundation — fix NAP, entity setup, basic schema
   - Week 2: Presence — content push, review generation
   - Week 3: Trust Signals — schema expansion, press, citations
   - Week 4: Optimization — authority wheel integration, reporting
8. **Role & Responsibilities** (15 min)
   - What client provides: access, content input, approvals
   - What you deliver: execution, monitoring, adjustments
   - Communication channels and frequency
9. **Q&A & Next Steps** (15 min)
   - Address concerns
   - Confirm first deliverable deadlines
   - Schedule weekly check-in calls

### Post-Call Actions
10. Send sprint roadmap document
11. Set up project management board (Trello/Asana/Notion)
12. Begin Week 1 tasks within 24 hours
13. Share recording/transcript of call

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] Sprint roadmap delivered
- [ ] Baseline scorecard shared
- [ ] Client access granted to tracking tools
- [ ] Week 1 tasks initiated

## Verification
Client confirms sprint plan acceptance
Dashboard reflects current baseline data
Week 1 tasks in progress
