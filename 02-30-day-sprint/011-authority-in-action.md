# SOP: Authority in Action
**Category:** 30 Day Sprint — Call 2 | **Duration:** 1h 20m

## Objective
Move from planning to execution — implement the first wave of authority-building actions across entity, content, and citation fronts.

## Prerequisites
- Sprint Kickoff Call completed (010)
- Baseline scorecard established
- Client access credentials provided

## Step-by-Step Process

### Pre-Call Prep
1. Review Week 1 progress: what tasks were completed, what stalled
2. Update scorecard with progress metrics
3. Prepare "quick wins" list — items completed in days, not weeks

### Call Agenda
4. **Week 1 Review** (15 min)
   - Celebrate quick wins completed
   - Address blockers
   - Adjust timeline if needed
5. **Entity Authority Actions** (20 min)
   - Confirm Wikidata entry in progress
   - Verify Knowledge Panel claim progress
   - Review NAP consistency sweep results
6. **Content Kickoff** (20 min)
   - Review content calendar
   - Approve first batch of topic clusters
   - Confirm AI-optimized writing guidelines
7. **Citation Expansion** (15 min)
   - Top 10 directory submission status
   - Industry-specific citation progress
   - Gap analysis for remaining directories
8. **Next Steps** (10 min)
   - Assign Week 2 tasks
   - Confirm deadline for next call

### Post-Call Actions
9. Submit any pending citation listings
10. Begin content creation per approved calendar
11. Schedule Week 3 progress check

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] Week 1 tasks complete or on track
- [ ] Content calendar approved
- [ ] Citation submissions in progress
- [ ] Updated progress scorecard

## Verification
Scorecard shows measurable progress on entity and citation spokes
Content production initiated
Client confirms satisfaction with pace
