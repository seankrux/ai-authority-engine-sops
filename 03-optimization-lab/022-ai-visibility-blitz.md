# SOP: AI Visibility Blitz Training
**Category:** Optimization Lab | **Duration:** 1h 57m

## Objective
Execute a high-intensity "blitz" campaign — a concentrated 7-14 day push across all authority channels to dramatically increase AI visibility in a short timeframe. Designed for launches, events, or recovery from visibility loss.

## Prerequisites
- All foundation SOPs in place (Lessons 1-6)
- Authority Wheel operational (008)
- Dashboard ready to track before/after (014)

## Step-by-Step Process

### Pre-Blitz Preparation (Days -7 to -1)
1. Set blitz objectives:
   - Target: +X% AI visibility score
   - Target: Y new citations
   - Target: Z new reviews
   - Target: Q AI engines where brand appears
2. Prepare assets in advance:
   - 10 social media posts (pre-scheduled)
   - 3 blog posts (draft + ready to publish)
   - 1 press release (draft)
   - 5 FAQ schema updates
   - Citation list for new submissions
3. Run baseline:
   - AI visibility score (018)
   - Traditional rankings (019)
   - Review counts across platforms

### Blitz Week 1: Foundation Surge
4. **Day 1**: NAP sweep — fix all citation discrepancies
5. **Day 2**: Wikidata update — add 5 new statements with references
6. **Day 3**: Schema push — add 3 new schema types to website
7. **Day 4**: Review request campaign — send to 20+ past clients
8. **Day 5**: Content publish — 2 blog posts with FAQ schema
9. **Day 6**: Directory submissions — 10 new citations
10. **Day 7**: Press release distribution

### Blitz Week 2: Visibility Push
11. **Day 8**: Social engagement blitz — boost 2 top posts
12. **Day 9**: Reddit — publish 2 high-value posts
13. **Day 10**: LinkedIn article + Medium cross-post
14. **Day 11**: YouTube video (transcript optimized)
15. **Day 12**: AI search audit — re-run live searches, measure lift
16. **Day 13**: Fix all AI accuracy issues found in audit
17. **Day 14**: Blitz report — before/after comparison

### Post-Blitz
18. Present blitz results: visibility score lift, new citations, new reviews
19. Maintain momentum: continue week 2 strategies at reduced intensity
20. Document what worked best per channel → inform next blitz
21. Schedule next blitz (quarterly)

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] Pre-blitz baseline captured
- [ ] 14-day blitz executed on schedule
- [ ] Post-blitz results measured and documented
- [ ] Blitz playbook updated with learnings

## Verification
AI visibility score improved by 20%+
10+ new citations created
10+ new reviews generated
Brand now visible in 2+ additional AI engines
