# SOP: Realtor Review Revelations
**Category:** Optimization Lab | **Duration:** 1h 42m

## Objective
Master the specific review and reputation strategies for real estate professionals — handling the unique challenges of agent-level vs brokerage-level review profiles, MLS data, and realtor-specific platforms.

## Prerequisites
- General review SOP established (004)
- Google Business Profile for real estate practice

## Step-by-Step Process

### Realtor Review Landscape
1. Map review platforms for real estate:
   - Google Business Profile (agent-level + brokerage-level)
   - Zillow (agent profile, reviews from clients)
   - Realtor.com
   - Facebook Recommendations
   - Yelp
   - Industry-specific: RateMyAgent, HomeLight, Redfin
2. Understand the dual-profile dynamic:
   - Personal agent GBP (for individual agent)
   - Brokerage GBP (for team/office)
   - Strategy: reviews on both, but differentiate content

### Review Generation for Realtors
3. Timing strategy:
   - Request review at: closing → 48h after keys delivered
   - Second touch: 30-day post-move check-in
   - Third touch: anniversary of closing
4. Script templates:
   - Post-closing: "Hi [Name], thrilled we found your perfect home. Would you share your experience? [Google link] [Zillow link]"
   - Milestone: "Happy 1 year in your new home! If you'd like to share how it's been..."
5. Video testimonial requests: offer to record 60s video review

### Managing Real Estate Reviews
6. Respond to ALL reviews within 24h
   - Positive: personalized thank you, mention specific detail
   - Negative: acknowledge, explain (without violating confidentiality), offer offline
7. Flag suspicious reviews to platform immediately
8. Leverage positive reviews in listing pages and open house materials

### AI Impact for Realtors
9. Real estate AI search prioritizes: review count + recency + response rate
10. Target: 50+ reviews across platforms, 4.8+ average
11. Use review snippets in schema markup on agent/listing pages
12. Link reviews to Google Knowledge Panel via aggregation

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] Agent + brokerage review strategy documented
- [ ] Review request sequences for each platform
- [ ] Response templates for all scenarios
- [ ] Review count targets set (50+ across platforms)

## Verification
Google shows review stars in search results (rich snippet)
Zillow profile has 10+ recent reviews
AI search results include positive review data in responses
