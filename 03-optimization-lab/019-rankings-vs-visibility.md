# SOP: Rankings vs. Visibility
**Category:** Optimization Lab | **Duration:** 1h 42m

## Objective
Understand the critical difference between traditional keyword rankings and AI visibility — and build strategies that optimize for the new AI-driven search paradigm, not just Google Page 1.

## Prerequisites
- Live Search SOP complete (018)
- Traditional rank tracking in place (Semrush/Ahrefs)

## Step-by-Step Process

### Understanding the Shift
1. The three types of search visibility:
   - **Traditional Rankings**: position 1-10 in Google SERPs
   - **AI Overview Visibility**: being cited in Google's AI-generated answers
   - **Generative Engine Visibility**: appearing in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude responses
2. Why traditional rankings don't equal AI visibility:
   - AI engines prioritize entity authority over keyword density
   - Zero-click searches mean top organic positions may still deliver zero traffic
   - AI often synthesizes from multiple sources, not just rank 1

### Audit: Rankings vs Visibility
3. Create dual tracking sheet:
   - Column A: keyword
   - Column B: traditional rank (Google, position)
   - Column C: AI answer inclusion (Y/N)
   - Column D: AI accuracy score (1-5)
   - Column E: AI source URL (what page did the AI cite?)
4. Run for top 20 keywords
5. Identify anomalies: "rank 1 but not in AI" vs "rank 10+ but cited by AI"

### Optimization Strategy
6. For keywords where rank = good but AI = missing:
   - Add FAQ schema targeting that query
   - Create direct answer paragraph (first 40-60 words)
   - Build entity authority signals around that topic
7. For keywords where AI = present but rank = low:
   - Improve traditional SEO fundamentals
   - Build backlinks to AI-cited page
   - Update content freshness
8. For keywords where both = bad:
   - Consider if this keyword is worth pursuing
   - Create dedicated content pillar with entity focus

### Reporting
9. Present dual metrics to clients: "You're visible in AI even without rank 1"
10. Track visibility share: % of top 20 keywords appearing in AI
11. Set targets: +10% AI visibility per month

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] Rankings vs Visibility audit complete

- [ ] New client reporting format with AI visibility metrics
