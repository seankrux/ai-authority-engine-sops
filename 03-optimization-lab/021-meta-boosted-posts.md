# SOP: Meta Boosted Posts
**Category:** Optimization Lab | **Duration:** 1h 12m

## Objective
Develop a paid social strategy (Facebook/Instagram/Meta ads) that generates AI-visible social proof signals — engagement, shares, comments — while driving real business results.

## Prerequisites
- Facebook Business Page active
- Meta Business Suite access
- Ad budget: $100-$1000/month recommended

## Step-by-Step Process

### Social Proof & AI Authority
1. Why boosted posts matter for AI:
   - Social signals (shares, engagement) feed brand authority signals
   - AI engines index public Facebook/Instagram content
   - High-engagement posts demonstrate brand relevance
   - Social profiles appear in Knowledge Panel → verified by AI

### Post Selection for Boosting
2. Choose posts that generate authority signals:
   - **Educational**: "How to [solve problem]" infographics
   - **Social proof**: "X clients served, Y reviews" milestone posts
   - **Behind the scenes**: team photos, office culture
   - **Client success**: before/after stories (with permission)
   - **Community involvement**: charity, events, local partnerships
   - **Thought leadership**: industry insights, trends predictions
3. Avoid: hard-sell, discount announcements, overly promotional content

### Boost Setup
4. Create campaign per post:
   - Objective: Engagement (shares + comments + reactions > clicks)
   - Audience: lookalike of best customers or location-based targeting
   - Budget: $10-30/day per post
   - Duration: 5-7 days per campaign
5. Ad creative best practices:
   - Video/image: high quality, brand colors
   - Text: short, question-based, value-first
   - CTA: "Learn More" or "Share" (not "Buy Now" for authority)

### Engagement Management
6. Respond to ALL comments within 2 hours
7. React to active comment threads → boost algorithmic reach
8. Share user-generated content from boosted posts
9. Repurpose top-performing organic posts as boosted content

### Measurement
10. Track: shares, comments, profile visits, website clicks
11. Report: engagement rate, cost per engagement, new followers
12. Compare boosted vs organic performance
13. Feed engagement metrics into authority dashboard

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] Boost-worthy post library (10+ candidate posts)
- [ ] Active boosted campaigns (2-3 concurrent)
- [ ] Comment response system active
- [ ] Engagement metrics tracked weekly

## Verification
Engagement rate > 3% on boosted posts
Social profiles appearing in Knowledge Panel
Content being shared across platforms (organic + paid cross-pollination)
