# SOP: Building Press Releases and Brand Mentions
**Category:** Getting Started — Lesson 6 | **Duration:** 1h 32m

## Objective
Leverage press releases and strategic brand mentions to build authoritative backlinks, citation trust, and AI-recognized brand signals.

## Prerequisites
- Schema data implemented (006)
- Newsworthy content ready: launches, milestones, awards, partnerships

## Step-by-Step Process

### Press Release Strategy
1. Identify newsworthy angles:
   - Product/service launch
   - Milestone (X clients served, Y years)
   - Industry award or recognition
   - Community involvement
   - Partnership announcement
   - Original research/data release
2. Write press release following AP style:
   - Headline: keyword-rich, under 100 chars
   - Lead paragraph: who, what, when, where, why (the full story)
   - Body: quotes, data, context
   - Boilerplate: standard company description (3-4 sentences)
   - Media contact: name, email, phone

### Distribution
3. Submit to tiered distribution networks:
   - **Tier 1** (premium): PRWeb, BusinessWire, GlobeNewswire ($200+)
   - **Tier 2** (mid): PRLog, EIN Presswire, Newswire ($50-200)
   - **Tier 3** (free): PR.com, PRFree, 1888PressRelease
4. Target industry-specific outlets alongside general distribution
5. Include 2-3 relevant links in press release body

### Brand Mention Acquisition
6. Set up HARO (Help a Reporter Out) alert for your expertise keywords
7. Respond to journalist queries within 2 hours (early bird advantage)
8. Create "expert source" profiles on: SourceBottle, ProfNet, Qwoted
9. Offer guest posts to industry publications (authority backlinks)
10. Monitor unlinked brand mentions and request link inclusion

### AI Authority from Coverage
11. Feature press mentions on website as trust signals
12. Link coverage to your brand's Wikidata/Wikipedia entries
13. Add press mentions to your Knowledge Panel supporting citations
14. Quote press coverage in AI-optimized content pages

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] 2+ press releases distributed
- [ ] HARO/response profiles active
- [ ] Press mentions section on website
- [ ] 5+ new authoritative backlinks from distribution

## Verification
Press release indexed and appearing for branded search
Brand mentioned in 3+ authoritative publications
AI engines reference press coverage in organic results
