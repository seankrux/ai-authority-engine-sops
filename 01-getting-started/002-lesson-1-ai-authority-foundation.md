# SOP: Building Your AI Authority Foundation
**Category:** Getting Started — Lesson 1 | **Duration:** 1h 54m

## Objective
Establish the foundational layer of AI authority — the core identity, entity signals, and trust infrastructure that all AI systems use to verify your brand's legitimacy.

## Prerequisites
- Completed AI Search Transformation Webinar (001)
- Website with basic SEO setup
- Business registered with consistent legal name

## Step-by-Step Process

### Step 1: Entity Identity Setup
1. Search your brand name in Google → note Knowledge Panel status
2. **If no panel exists:** Submit to Wikidata via https://www.wikidata.org
   - Create item with: legal name, description, website, founding date, industry
   - Add Wikipedia-style references where possible
3. **If panel exists:** Audit accuracy — check hours, phone, address, category
4. Claim your Google Knowledge Panel (if unclaimed): search "knowledge panel claim" → follow Google's verification flow

### Step 2: NAP Consistency Sweep
5. Export all current citations: Yelp, YellowPages, Facebook, Bing, Apple Maps
6. Cross-reference Name, Address, Phone — flag every discrepancy
7. Fix inconsistencies using Moz Local, BrightLocal, or manual dashboard edits
8. Prioritize: Google Business Profile → Apple Maps → Bing → Facebook → Top 10 industry directories

### Step 3: AI Trust Signals
9. Install organization schema on homepage (JSON-LD format)
10. Add sameAs property pointing to all verified social profiles
11. Ensure HTTPS, fast load time (Core Web Vitals pass)
12. Create an "About" page with complete entity description (50+ words)

### Step 4: Monitoring Foundation
13. Set up Google Alerts for brand name + common misspellings
14. Configure Semrush Brand Monitoring (or alternative)
15. Schedule weekly authority audit (15 min)

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] Wikidata entry created/updated
- [ ] Knowledge Panel claimed and accurate
- [ ] NAP 100% consistent across top 10 directories
- [ ] Organization schema installed on homepage

## Verification
Google search your brand → Knowledge Panel shows accurate data
Semrush Authority Score baseline captured
No NAP discrepancies in citation audit
