# SOP: The AI Authority Wheel
**Category:** Getting Started — Bonus 1 | **Duration:** 2h 09m

## Objective
Build and implement the AI Authority Wheel — a holistic framework connecting all authority signals (entity, content, schema, reviews, citations, PR, social proof) into one integrated AI visibility system.

## Prerequisites
- Lessons 1-6 completed
- Schema, reviews, press, content, presence layers all live

## Step-by-Step Process

### Understanding the Authority Wheel
1. The 7 spokes of AI Authority:
   - **Entity** — Knowledge Panel, Wikidata, Wikipedia
   - **Content** — Pillar pages, blogs, videos
   - **Schema** — Structured data markup
   - **Reviews** — Google, third-party, testimonials
   - **Citations** — NAP consistency, directory listings
   - **Press/PR** — Brand mentions, coverage
   - **Social Proof** — Following, engagement, shares

### Wheel Assembly
2. Create the Authority Hub page — central hub linking all 7 spokes
3. On each spoke page, link back to the Hub and reference 2-3 other spokes
4. Schema relationship: ensure all spoke entities @id-reference each other
5. Cross-pollinate content: quote reviews in blog posts, cite articles in press releases

### Spoke Strength Analysis
6. Score each spoke 1-10 based on current state:
   - Entity: Knowledge Panel active? Wikidata entry? (score)
   - Content: 10+ authoritative pages? Updated regularly? (score)
   - Schema: All tier 1-2 types deployed? (score)
   - Reviews: 20+ reviews, 4.5+ stars? Recent? (score)
   - Citations: 100% NAP consistent? 20+ directories? (score)
   - Press: 5+ mentions? Authority domains? (score)
   - Social Proof: Active following? Engagement rate? (score)
7. Identify weakest spoke → focus next sprint there

### Ongoing Wheel Maintenance
8. Monthly spoke audit — rescore each spoke
9. Rotate focus: each month strengthen 1-2 spokes
10. Track correlations: does review increase → better AI responses?
11. Maintain Authority Hub page with latest scores visible

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] Authority Hub page live
- [ ] All 7 spokes scored and documented
- [ ] Weakest spoke identified and improvement plan in place
- [ ] Monthly rescore calendar scheduled

## Verification
All 7 spokes scoring 7+ out of 10
AI engines reference multiple authority signals in responses
Authority Wheel integrated into client reporting
