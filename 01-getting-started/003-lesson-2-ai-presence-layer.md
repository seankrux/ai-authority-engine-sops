# SOP: Building Your AI Presence Layer
**Category:** Getting Started — Lesson 2 | **Duration:** 2h 13m

## Objective
Establish a comprehensive AI presence layer — the digital footprint across AI answer engines, LLM training data, and generative search platforms that determines how AI represents your brand.

## Prerequisites
- AI Authority Foundation complete (002)
- At least 5 active social profiles with consistent branding

## Step-by-Step Process

### Phase 1: AI Platform Presence Audit
1. Query your brand + primary keyword across: ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, Copilot
2. Document how each AI describes your brand — note inaccuracies, missing info, competitor mentions
3. Check if your brand appears in AI-generated "best of" lists and recommendation queries
4. Screenshot each AI response for baseline comparison

### Phase 2: Content Saturation Strategy
5. Create high-authority content hubs per service area (3-5 pages min)
6. Publish to platforms indexed by AI training data:
   - LinkedIn Articles (long-form, 800+ words)
   - Medium (repurpose with canonical tags)
   - YouTube (transcripts get indexed)
   - Reddit (Q&A style, subreddit-specific)
   - Quora (answer questions with authority)
7. Ensure every piece links back to your site (contextual, natural)

### Phase 3: Structured Data for AI Discovery
8. Add Article schema to all blog/content pages
9. Implement FAQ schema for common questions
10. Add HowTo schema for service walkthroughs
11. Use Product schema if selling services/products online

### Phase 4: Citation Expansion
12. Submit to AI-specific directories: Crunchbase, AngelList, G2, Capterra
13. Create/update Wikipedia-style pages on industry wikis
14. Add your brand to relevant "list" articles on authoritative sites

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] AI Presence Audit Report (baseline screenshots)
- [ ] 3+ high-authority content pieces published
- [ ] FAQ + Article schema deployed
- [ ] AI-specific directory profiles created

## Verification
AI engines consistently describe your brand accurately in 4/5 platforms tested
Content pieces indexed and appearing in AI search results
