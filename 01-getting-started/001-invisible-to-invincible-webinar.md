# SOP: Invisible to Invincible — The AI Search Transformation Webinar
**Category:** Getting Started | **Duration:** 3h 04m

## Objective
Transform your online presence from invisible to invincible using AI-powered search authority strategies. Understand the paradigm shift from traditional SEO to AI-driven visibility.

## Prerequisites
- Google Search Console access
- Google Business Profile (GBP) claimed
- Basic understanding of SEO concepts

## Step-by-Step Process

### Phase 1: AI Search Landscape Assessment
1. Run a "zero-click search" audit — search your core keywords without clicking
2. Identify which AI answer services (SGE, Perplexity, ChatGPT, Gemini) surface your brand
3. Document current visibility gaps using the AI Authority Matrix
4. Map keywords to AI answer types: direct answers, listicles, comparison tables

### Phase 2: Authority Foundation Setup
5. Claim and verify all knowledge graph entities (Google Knowledge Panel, Wikidata)
6. Install AI authority tracking tools (BrightLocal, Semrush AI features)
7. Create baseline scorecard across: visibility, citations, reviews, schema, content

### Phase 3: Transformation Roadmap
8. Prioritize quick wins: fix NAP consistency, add FAQ schema, claim unlisted profiles
9. Schedule weekly AI presence audits (15 min blocks)
10. Set up automated brand mention alerts (Google Alerts, Mention.com)

## Key Deliverables
- [ ] AI Visibility Baseline Scorecard
- [ ] Knowledge Graph Entity Check (claimed/unclaimed)
- [ ] Priority Action List (ranked by effort vs impact)

## Verification
- Your brand appears in at least 3/5 AI answer engines for primary keyword
- Knowledge Panel is active and accurate
- All citation sources agree on NAP data
